This mod adds several uniforms for the female multiplayer character model.

Police, Sheriff, Coast Guard, Armed Forces, FIB, Highway Patrol and finally the Park Ranger

The specifics are:

LSPD - A retextured shirt with the badge and name tag like the AI cops, a bulletproof vest with "police" to match the officers in game plus a baseball cap for more a casual look or ununiformed work.

LSSL - Retextured police shirt and trousers to match the sheriff uniform, a bulletproof vest with "police" again to match the in game ones (please don't be angry it's not sheriff) and a matching sheriff cowboy

USCG - A vest, trousers, polo shirt and a baseball cap to match the in game look.

USAF - A BDU consisting of trousers, a shirt and a pair of boots plus a two tone under body armour combat shirt.

FIB - A FIB bulletproof vest, a wondbreaker jacket and a baseball cap.

SAHP - Retexed shirt and 2 trousers (one for boots tucked in and one for... normal? patrols) and a highway patrol helmet.

SAPR - Retextured police shirt (trousers didn't need doing) and a baseball cap with the park ranger badge on it

Hope I remembered it all.
